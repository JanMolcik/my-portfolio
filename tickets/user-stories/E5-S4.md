## Goal
- As search user, I want writing index, pagination, and details to have deterministic metadata and sitemap coverage, so that blog/notes pages are discoverable.

## Scope
- Writing SEO, sitemap, JSON-LD, and canonical behavior; supports BW-005.
- Delivery aligned with G-B execution group.

## Non-goals
- Unrelated module rewrites.

## Acceptance
- `/writing`, `/writing/page/[page]`, and `/writing/[slug]` have central metadata/canonical behavior; sitemap includes `/writing` plus published details and only indexable paginated pages; detail JSON-LD includes modified date, image, tags/keywords, and article/blog posting data where available.
